Planar velocity deals with velocity on a 2D plane. There are multiple formulas related to this topic.

You can move the "ship" by pressing the arrow keys.

On this page, acceleration and speed are treated as scalars. The acceleration delta is added to the speed of the ship each frame. The velocity is calculated by multiplying the ship's direction unit vector with its speed. The position delta is then calculated from velocity and time and added to the previous position.
